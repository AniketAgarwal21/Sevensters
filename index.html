About

The Sevensters or The Seven Sisters also known as "Paradise Unexplored" is a name given to the states of Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Meghalaya, Manipur, Mizoram, Nagaland and Tripura in northeastern India. Talking about North-eastern India, one can only remember to mention the magical wonders of the nature, swinging of the wide range of trees in the breezing noises of the wind, awe-inspiring views of the natural landscape and best cultural sites and architectural structures that remind you of age-old traditions & culture. The seven states are the best examples of love, purity and bliss in the form of tourist attractions and endless beauty.
